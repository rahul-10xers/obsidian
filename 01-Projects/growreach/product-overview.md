## What Is GrowReach?

GrowReach is an AI Powered LinkedIn engagement automation SaaS that works as your LinkedIn co-pilot — 24/7 while you are busy living life.

It monitors the LinkedIn accounts you specify and searches LinkedIn for keywords you define. It tracks and identifies the most viral and relevant posts for you to comment on and even goes ahead and generates a thoughtful, detailed and human sounding comment that mentions your product/service and talks from the revelant experience you've had to show you as an authority on that topic. Then either you can copy and modify that comment manually or run growreach on auto-mode so it posts thoughtful, contextual comments automatically while you sleep.

**The result:** You show up consistently in the conversations your ideal customers are already having — building visibility, credibility, and inbound interest without spending hours scrolling LinkedIn every day.

---

## The Core Problem It Solves

Most professionals know commenting on LinkedIn works for building visibility and generating business. But:

- **It takes 1–3 hours a day** to find the right posts, craft good comments, and engage consistently
- **Consistency is the hard part** — most people start, then stop, and lose all momentum
- **Finding the right posts** to engage with is harder than writing the comment itself
- **Procrastintion and Chasing Perfection** the blank box effect where you overthink what to comment to sound smart and thoughtful.
- **Manual commenting doesn't scale** — you can't do 20+ quality comments a day without automation

GrowReach removes all three bottlenecks.

## How It Works (3 Steps)

1. **Set up your targets** — Add the LinkedIn accounts you want to monitor + the keywords relevant to your niche
2. **GrowReach finds the posts** — AI surfaces the highest-engagement, most relevant posts from both sources
3. **Comments go out** — Either you review and approve, or the system posts automatically. You check in when leads arrive.

## What GrowReach Is NOT

- **Not a post scheduler** — We're about engagement (comments), not content creation (posts). Though we do have a carousel generator as a bonus feature.
- **Not a connection spammer** — We don't send mass connection requests or cold DMs
- **Not generic automation** — Every comment is contextually generated from the actual post content, not a template
- **Not a LinkedIn scraper** — We work within LinkedIn's ecosystem to surface relevant conversations
- **Not a replacement for strategy** — GrowReach executes engagement. The strategy (which accounts to target, which keywords, what your positioning is) is still set by you.
