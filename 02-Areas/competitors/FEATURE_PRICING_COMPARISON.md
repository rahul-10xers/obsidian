### LigoSocial — ligosocial.com

**"The Only AI That Doesn't Sound Like AI"**

**Overview:** LigoSocial is the most direct philosophical competitor to GrowReach's vision of authentic engagement. They connect to your LinkedIn history to learn your actual writing voice, then generate posts and comments that are indistinguishable from your natural style. Their explicit anti-AI positioning ("93% of users say LiGo comments are indistinguishable from human-written") makes them the authenticity-first tool in the market.

**How it works:** Users connect their LinkedIn account. LiGo ingests their post history to build a voice model. The Chrome extension then surfaces in their LinkedIn feed to suggest comments on posts from their engagement lists. A lead management dashboard lets users save and track prospects one-click from any profile. Content recycling resurfaces their own top posts from prior quarters.

**Pricing:**
- Standard: ~$29/month
- Pro: ~$76/month
- $15 free credit trial (no credit card)
- Plans can be tried before committing

**What they do well:**
- Most authentic-sounding AI comments in the market — trained on YOUR actual writing
- Lead management integrated with commenting workflow
- Content recycling (re-engages your best old content)
- Strong trust positioning for professionals worried about "AI voice"
- Agency delegation tools for safe client account management

**Where they fall short:**
- No autonomous auto-posting — user must be present for every comment
- No keyword-based post discovery — relies on pre-curated engagement lists
- No 24/7 operation — requires active sessions
- Chrome extension dependency for key features
- No carousel generator or content creation beyond posts

**The key difference from GrowReach:**
LigoSocial is a *premium comment quality* play — it makes every comment you manually post feel more human. GrowReach is an *autonomous volume + targeting* play — it finds the right posts and posts comments while you sleep. They could theoretically be used together (LiGo for quality polish, GrowReach for reach and volume).

**Who switches to GrowReach:**
LigoSocial users who love the authentic AI voice but are frustrated that they still need to open LinkedIn daily to make it work.

**Who should stay with LigoSocial:**
Professionals for whom comment authenticity is the #1 priority and who are already spending time on LinkedIn daily anyway — they just want faster, better-sounding comments.

### Reepl — reepl.io

**"The All-in-One LinkedIn Co-Pilot That Never Leaves LinkedIn"**

**Overview:** Reepl is the most feature-packed Chrome extension in the market — it's essentially an entire LinkedIn stack (content, scheduling, commenting, prospecting, CRM, email finding) embedded natively in LinkedIn's UI without switching tabs. It targets users who want everything unified in one place, stay native to LinkedIn, and positions on "highest value-for-money."

**How it works:** Users install the Reepl Chrome extension. It overlays LinkedIn with a sidebar that shows AI comment suggestions on every post, a post composer with performance prediction, a prospecting panel for profile analysis, and a CRM-lite for saving leads. Content can be imported from YouTube/blogs/PDFs and repurposed into posts.

**Pricing (fetched via headless browser, March 2026):**

| Plan | Annual/mo | Free Trial |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | — |
| Solo Pro | $14/mo | 14 days, no CC |
| Solo Premium | $28/mo | 14 days, no CC |
| Solo Max | $74/mo | 14 days, no CC |

> Monthly billing adds ~50% on top (33% annual discount). Teams/Agency plans also available.

Free plan includes: 100 AI credits, 10 comments/day, 10 messages/day, 100 contacts, 2 lists.

**What they do well:**
- Most feature-rich Chrome extension — everything in one place
- Post performance prediction score before publishing (0–100)
- Email finder for LinkedIn connections
- Native CRM with HubSpot, Pipedrive, Notion, Google Sheets sync
- Growth gamification (streaks, daily scoreboards, weekly stats)
- Viral idea generator + carousel creator
- Forever free plan with no credit card

**Where they fall short:**
- No autonomous auto-posting — completely manual; user must be present
- All features require Chrome to be open
- Opaque pricing (not publicly listed — must sign up to see)
- No cloud operation; nothing happens when browser is closed
- Complexity may overwhelm users who just want commenting automation

**The key difference from GrowReach:**
Reepl is a co-pilot you fly yourself — it makes every action faster and smarter but YOU still do everything. GrowReach is an autonomous agent — it acts on your behalf without you needing to be present. Reepl is better if you enjoy engaging on LinkedIn and want to be more productive. GrowReach is better if you want LinkedIn results without spending time on LinkedIn.

**Who switches to GrowReach:**
Reepl free/paid users who realise they're still spending 30–60 minutes daily on LinkedIn and want that time back entirely.

**Who should stay with Reepl:**
Active LinkedIn users who want the richest set of AI-assisted tools while they manually scroll, combined with prospecting/CRM in one place. Also users who need email finding.

### Engage AI — engage-ai.co

**Two Products, One Brand — Know Which You're Buying**

**Overview:** Engage AI is unusual: the same domain hosts two fundamentally different products. The homepage promotes a *human engagement pod community* (real people comment on your posts). Their `/pricing` page is a completely different AI comment monitoring and generation tool. Buyers need to know which product they're evaluating.

---

**Product A: Engage AI Human Pods**

A structured network of verified professionals who manually comment on each other's posts. When you publish, 15 community members are assigned to leave thoughtful comments over 3 staggered days. No bots — all human. Timezone cohorts: APAC, Europe/UK, USA East.

**Pricing:**
- Individual: $50/month
- Group (unlimited clients): $250/month

**What it does well:**
- Real human comments — highest authenticity possible, zero LinkedIn risk
- Staggered delivery mimics organic engagement perfectly
- Accountability system (non-participators get removed)
- Works for company LinkedIn pages too

**Where it falls short:**
- Most expensive per-outcome in the category
- You MUST manually comment on others' posts in return — significant time commitment
- Doesn't help you comment on other people's posts for visibility — only boosts your own
- Not scalable for agencies (you need real humans participating)
- $250/month agency plan still depends on human bandwidth

---

**Product B: Engage AI Comment Generator (AI tool)**

Monitors a list of target prospects on LinkedIn. When a prospect posts, it alerts you and generates a contextual AI comment for you to review and post. Designed for sales professionals tracking target accounts, not for mass visibility building.

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Monthly | Annual |
|------|---------|--------|
| Free | $0 | — |
| Starter | $12.90 | $10.32/mo |
| Pro | $30 | $24/mo |
| Elite | $80 | $64/mo |

Free plan: unlimited comments, 3 tones, 10 prospects, weekly updates.
14-day free trial of any paid plan. 30-day money-back guarantee.

**What it does well:**
- Cheapest paid entry in the category ($10.32/mo annual)
- Multiple AI models on higher tiers (GPT-4o, 4.5, Gemini)
- Prospect-first workflow — built around sales pipeline, not general visibility
- Available on Microsoft AppSource (enterprise trust signal)
- Generous free plan

**Where it falls short:**
- No post discovery — only monitors pre-specified prospects
- Requires manual action to post comments (no autonomy)
- Sales-oriented (tracks prospects, not viral posts in your niche)
- No carousel, scheduling, or content creation

**The key difference from GrowReach:**
Engage AI's tool is outbound-oriented (I want to engage with specific people I'm trying to sell to). GrowReach is visibility-oriented (I want to show up in conversations my ideal audience is already having). Both involve AI comments, but the strategy is completely different.

**Who switches to GrowReach:**
Engage AI users who want broad visibility beyond their prospect list — they want to reach people who don't know them yet, not just warm up known leads.

**Who should stay with Engage AI:**
Sales professionals with a defined target account list who want AI assistance for account-based engagement specifically.

It auto-generates hyper-personalized connection requests, follow-up messages, and even voice notes based on each prospect's profile and recent activity. The free residential proxies per account are a notable differentiator for agencies running multi-account operations safely.

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Monthly | Annual |
|------|---------|--------|
| Starter | $29/account | $19/account |
| Professional | $59/month | $39/month |
| Enterprise | $79/month | $49/month |

Free tier available with unlimited AI personalization, campaigns, and contacts.

**What they do well:**
- Voice note automation (unique in the market — automated voice messages)
- Free residential proxies per account (strong safety feature for agencies)
- AI reads each prospect's recent posts to personalize outreach (not template-based)
- Unified inbox with account rotation for multi-account management
- Unlimited campaigns and contacts on all plans

**Where they fall short:**
- Completely different use case — no commenting, no visibility building
- Outbound cold messages carry LinkedIn ToS risk if volume is too high
- Not for personal brand visibility — entirely focused on outbound sales pipeline

**The key difference from GrowReach:**
Prosp is cold outreach automation (send personalized messages to strangers to generate leads). GrowReach is warm visibility building (comment on viral posts so your ideal audience discovers you organically). These are genuinely different strategies — one is push (Prosp), one is pull (GrowReach). A sales professional might use BOTH: Prosp for outbound sequences, GrowReach for ambient personal brand building.

**Who switches to GrowReach:**
Prosp users who've experienced LinkedIn account warnings from high-volume cold outreach and want a safer, organic way to build pipeline through inbound interest.

**Who should stay with Prosp:**
Sales teams doing structured outbound prospecting who need personalized message automation at scale.
