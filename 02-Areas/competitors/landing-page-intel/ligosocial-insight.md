## 1. POSITIONING OVERVIEW

**Core positioning:** "LinkedIn Second Brain" — AI that learns your voice in 3 minutes, lets you delegate to a VA with confidence
**H1:** "LiGo helps you grow on LinkedIn Organically & Authentically"
**Primary value prop:** Trains on your voice from your LinkedIn profile URL in 3 minutes, produces content at 93% voice accuracy — "the only AI text generation tool that doesn't sound like AI"
**Target ICP:** Agencies (multi-client voice management), solopreneurs (delegate to VA), busy professionals (engagement at scale)
**Key differentiator vs category:** Voice authenticity ("93% indistinguishable from human-written") + VA delegation as a first-class feature
**Product delivery:** Chrome extension (primary) + web app
**Safety claim:** "LinkedIn ToS Compliant (suggestions, not automation)" — same manual-approval positioning as Reepl, NOT GrowReach-style automation

## 5. PRICING STRUCTURE

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Standard** | $29/mo | $24/mo (-17%) | 300 AI credits/month, Chrome extension, basic analytics |
| **Pro** | $76/mo | $63/mo (-17%) | 1,200 AI credits, ChatGPT & Claude integration, CRM, deep analytics, delegate access |
| **Ghostwriting** | $1,999/mo | $1,599/mo (-20%) | Done-for-you, 4-8 posts/week, dedicated account manager |

**Free entry mechanic:** "$15 free credit, no credit card required" — **credit-based free trial**, not time-limited. Credits are consumed by AI actions; lighter actions use fewer credits.

> This is smarter than ConnectSafely's 7-day trial — the credit-based model gives users a natural reason to upgrade (credits run out based on usage, not an arbitrary clock). User only upgrades when they've experienced value.

**Additional credit purchase:** "Credits that stack with your plan and never expire" — upsell path for power users.

## 11. PRODUCT DIFFERENTIATION (key insight for GrowReach)

**LigoSocial is NOT competing with GrowReach.** Their product is AI-assisted content creation, not engagement pod automation.

| Feature | LigoSocial | ConnectSafely | GrowReach |
|---------|-----------|--------------|-----------|
| Comment posting | AI suggests → user approves | Fully automated | Fully automated |
| Post boosting | None — scheduling only | Engagement pods (real people) | Engagement pods |
| Voice training | 3-min LinkedIn URL analysis | None | None |
| VA delegation | Core feature (built for it) | None | None |
| Primary delivery | Chrome extension | Web app | Web app |
| Pricing entry | $15 free credits (no card) | $19/mo | ? |
| Ban safety claim | "ToS Compliant — not automation" | "SafeToS" approval | ? |

LiGo serves professionals who want **authentic voice AI assistance**. GrowReach serves professionals who want **automated LinkedIn reach amplification**. The overlap customer is someone who uses BOTH: LiGo to create content, GrowReach to boost it.
