## ACQUISITION CHANNELS BY GROWTH STAGE

### PRE-LAUNCH (Before product exists)

**Pre-Sale LTD Validation [Gil, Subscribr]**
- Built email list of 1000+ on X via viral giveaways
- Sold 50 lifetime licenses at "absurdly low" price
- Made $20K in 2-3 days BEFORE building the product
- Used pre-sale revenue to fund 3 months of development
- Tags: [WORKED] [LOW-COST] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

**$5 Queue-Jump Validation [Giovanni Brees, MeetOscar]**
- Created 200 landing page variations for 15 target audiences using Lovable
- Ran Meta Ads with <$5K budget
- Required $5 prepayment to join waitlist (credit card = real commitment)
- Discovered lawyers converted best while support agents (original hypothesis) were too slow
- Result: $45K MRR in 60 days
- Tags: [WORKED] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

**Equity-for-Distribution [Hassam, Launch Fast]**
- Identified coaching program (Legacy X) with thousands of Amazon sellers
- Pitched: "Give me 48 hours to build a better tool"
- Traded 50% equity for instant access to their customer base
- Built MVP using Cursor, Vercel, Supabase, Apify in 48 hours
- Result: $21.8K MRR in 90 days
- Tags: [WORKED] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

**Fake Sales Call Validation [Alex Becker, Hyros]**
- Drew rudimentary Excel dashboard concept
- Conducted simulated sales calls asking "Would you pay $3,000 upfront annually?"
- Received repeated "Yes" responses before writing any code
- Tags: [WORKED] [LOW-COST] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

### 10→100 USERS

**Reddit Storytelling [Roman, Goji Berry AI]**
- Posted high-transparency stories in r/SaaS, r/Entrepreneur
- Format: "I paid 5 influencers on LinkedIn. Here's what $1,250 got me" (160K views)
- Warmed account 7-14 days with comments only
- Used one account per browser (Chrome, Safari, Mozilla) to avoid footprinting
- Activated "hide feed" so users can't see promotion history
- Never put SaaS link in post; triggered curiosity → profile link
- Result: 11M impressions, 40K visitors, $30K MRR in 5 months
- Tags: [WORKED] [LOW-COST] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

**Reddit Case Studies [Diego, App Alchemi]**

- Result: 1M+ views, $17K MRR in 4 months with zero initial audience
- Tags: [WORKED] [LOW-COST] [SOLO-FRIENDLY]

- Buy hundreds of secondary domains

- Tools: Instantly, Smartlead, Apollo, ZoomInfo, Clay

- Offer white-glove setup justifying annual contract ($3,500+)
- 100% money-back guarantee
- The 20% Rule: If product saves $10K, charge $2K
- Result: $40M ARR bootstrapped
- Tags: [WORKED] [SCALABLE]

## PRICING TACTICS

**LTD-to-Subscription Bridge [Mike, Frill; Gil, Subscribr; Nick, BlockToPin]**
- Launch private LTD to raise $30K-$100K cash
- Use cash to fund 1-2 years of SEO/content while transitioning to MRR
- Nick: Launched LTD first, switched to $39/mo subscription after 10 paying customers
- Mike: Never give away free accounts; payment ensures usage and better feedback

**Capped Beta Cohorts [Rob Hoffman, Cleo]**
- Beta 1: $59 LTD (500 spots), Beta 2: $79 LTD, Public: $99/mo
- Constrained supply makes demand pull the product
- "The less information shared, the more demand grew"

**BYOK Cost Elimination [Prrenit, Canvas Mode]**
- Desktop app where users bring their own API keys
- Zero ongoing COGS; allows profitable LTDs
- Pivoted from failing subscription web app → $80K revenue in 6 months

**Annual Upfront Contracts [Alex Becker, Hyros]**
- White-glove setup justifies annual commitment
- Drops churn from 15-20% to 3-5%
- Creates instant cash flow for bootstrapping

**Feature Bidding [Giovanni Brees, MeetOscar]**
- Users requesting features move to top of priority list if they switch to yearly subscription
- Drives annual conversion while prioritizing engaged users

## RETENTION & ACTIVATION TACTICS

**Founder-as-Support [Tibo]**
- Until $10K MRR, "support" link directs to founder's Twitter DMs
- Fix bugs in 5-10 minutes of user DM → "customers for life"
- Use own products daily to catch UX annoyances

**White-Glove Onboarding [Alex Becker, Hyros]**
- Manually set up product for first 50 customers
- Treat onboarding as separate product from the software
- Transition to Head of Customer Success after 100 customers

**Screen Recording Reviews [Nick, BlockToPin]**
- Review FullStory/PostHog recordings of user sessions
- Build requested features in hours/days
- "Snowball Effect": Extreme care → recommendations → organic growth

**Negative Friction Retention [Lots, Pray Screen]**
- App blocks social media; user must pray to unlock
- Result: 60% Day-30 retention ("unheard of")

**Capped Beta Cohorts for Onboarding [Rob Hoffman, Cleo]**
- Launch to 500 users max to handle feedback without overwhelming
- VIP onboarding calls; gave personal phone number to every user

**Activation Metrics:**
- Becker (Hyros): 15% increase in tracked ad revenue → churn drops to 3-5%
- Lots (Pray Screen): Prayer completion → 60% Day-30 retention
- Tibo: Recurring daily usage
- Nick (BlockToPin): One month of pins scheduled in minutes
- Margot: Invoice generated in <30 seconds → 2.5x conversion increase

## SEO TACTICS

**Bottom-of-Funnel (BoFU) Strategy [Rob Hoffman; Aayush, Elephas]**
- Focus on "Alternatives to [Competitor]" and "[A] vs [B]" pages
- Target KD < 20, Search Volume > 500
- Aayush: Generated $70K in one year from BoFU Google traffic
- Tools: Ahrefs, Semrush, Neuron Writer

**Language Arbitrage [Rob Hoffman]**
- Take proven English SaaS concept → build native version for non-English language
- Execute SEO in that language (much lower competition — "sea of green")
- Example: Teach Easy (French Teachable) → $65K MRR

**AI Search Optimization / LLMO**
- Create hyper-comprehensive comparison pages that AI models cite
- Tally (form builder): 17x higher conversion from AI search vs traditional Google

**Programmatic SEO [Gil, Subscribr]**
- Automated/templated page creation at scale for long-tail keywords
- Combined with pre-sale and email list for $30K MRR at 4000+ customers

## FAILURE PATTERNS (What NOT to do)

**Building in Silence [Loic, Rob Hallum]**
- Loic: 5 years shipping features without user conversations → failure
- Rob Hallum: 2.5 years of failed products before building in public

**Vague Messaging [Margot]**
- "Future of productivity" headlines → 2.5x lower conversion than benefit-first headlines

**Low-Ticket Mass Market via Ads [Alex Becker]**
- "Selling $50/month subscriptions via ads is f***ed" — CAC too high, churn 10%+

**Free Accounts Without Commitment [Mike]**
- "Never give away an account for free" — free users provide worse feedback

**Founders Expansion Disorder [Giovanni Brees]**
- Hiring low-budget employees instead of "A-players" keeps founder stuck as employee

**Ignoring Token Costs for LTDs [Rob Hoffman]**
- AI startups must "do the math" on token costs before selling lifetime access

## MENTAL MODELS

- **Painkiller vs Vitamin [Gil]:** Only build painkillers — products solving must-fix problems
- **Results-as-a-Service [Becker]:** Manually perform the task your software will do for first customers
- **Edgy Sales [Rob Hoffman]:** Lead with spicy content; plug product subtly in comments
- **4-3-2-1 Framework [Lara Costa]:** 4 posts/week, 3 pillars, 2 audiences (ICP and IFP), 1 CTA
- **The Gossip Effect [Margot]:** Email two decision-makers; mention you contacted the other
- **20% Rule [Becker]:** If product saves $10K, charge $2K (20% of value created)
- **Quit as Fast as You Ship [Mark Lou via Hoffman]:** If no traction in 48 hours, move on
- **Language Arbitrage [Hoffman]:** Clone proven US SaaS for non-English language SEO
- **80/20 of SEO [Hoffman]:** Only BoFU pages: Alternatives, Versus, comparisons
- **Vibe Coding [Brees]:** Use AI tools (Lovable/Cursor) to build MVPs in 48 hours
